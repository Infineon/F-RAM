This repository contains QSPI Driver support for NXP' LPC18XX nad 43XX controllers from ST Micro electronics.
Further details can be found on our blog at following address: https://community.cypress.com/community/memory/nonvolatile-ram/blog/2018/03/15/quad-spi-excelon-ultra-f-ram-driver-support-for-nxp-microcontroller-lpc1857-series
